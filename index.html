About IPPS

IPPS is a dedicated industrial filtration distributor focused on liquid and process-critical applications. Our portfolio includes filter housings, plate and frame systems, lenticular formats, depth and membrane cartridges, filter media, bag filters, and vessels.

From Puerto Rico, we support buyers across the Caribbean with practical guidance, reliable availability, and the operational responsiveness procurement teams need to keep plants moving.
