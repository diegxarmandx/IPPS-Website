About IPPS

IPPS is an industrial filtration distributor focused on liquid and process filtration products. Our catalog includes filter housings, plate and frame systems, filter media, lenticular formats, pleated cartridges, depth cartridges, liquid filter bags, and filter vessels.

From Puerto Rico, we support customers across the Caribbean with reliable availability and practical product guidance so buyers can source the right filtration solution quickly and confidently.

Our supply footprint is built for Caribbean logistics. We support operations in Puerto Rico and neighboring markets with dependable turnaround and clear communication for procurement teams.
